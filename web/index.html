About this chatbot

This chatbot searches across all 363 episodes of The Grey NATO podcast using RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation). Your question is matched against episode transcripts, synopses, and 7,663 curated shownotes links using both vector similarity search and full-text keyword search. The best matching excerpts are fed to a local LLM (running in LM Studio) which generates a grounded answer with episode citations. All data comes from transcripts produced by tgn-whisperer.

Try these sample queries:

  • What do Jason and James think of Girard-Perregaux?
  • Which episodes feature guest appearances?
  • What are the best field watches discussed on the show?
  • Tell me about the trips to Japan
